About

Mobility for the digital era

Getting around should be simple (and even fun!) for everyone. We embrace technology to provide low cost, smart access to scooters at your fingertips.

Better urban living

We’re helping connect cities and bring people closer together. Our scooters are also fully-electric and we offset the minimal carbon footprint for each.
